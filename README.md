# Marketing Campaign Conversion Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes marketing campaign performance and customer behavior to identify the key factors driving customer conversion. Using exploratory data analysis (EDA) and machine learning techniques, the analysis investigates campaign effectiveness, customer engagement patterns, loyalty behavior, and conversion drivers.

## Business Questions

1. Which channels and campaign types generate the highest conversion?
2. Which customer groups are most likely to convert?
3. What behaviors indicate higher conversion?
4. Does higher advertising spend improve conversion?
5. Which campaign combinations perform best?
6. Are loyal customers more likely to convert?
7. Do email opens and email clicks drive conversion?
8. What factors have strong or weak relationships with conversion?
9. Can customer conversion be predicted using machine learning?

## Dataset

The dataset contains customer demographics, campaign information, engagement metrics, loyalty indicators, and conversion outcomes.

Key variables include:

- Age, Gender, Income
- Campaign Channel and Campaign Type
- Ad Spend and Click-Through Rate
- Website Visits, Pages Per Visit, Time On Site
- Email Opens and Email Clicks
- Previous Purchases and Loyalty Points
- Conversion (Target Variable)

## Analysis Performed
- Exploratory Data Analysis

## Business Recommendations
- Increase website engagement initiatives to improve conversion rates.
- Prioritize customers demonstrating strong engagement signals.
- Strengthen loyalty programs to encourage repeat conversions.
- Improve email marketing effectiveness through higher engagement strategies.
- Focus on behavioral targeting rather than demographic segmentation alone.
